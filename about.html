About Us

Location Services is a Santa Monica‑based location scouting and management company serving the film, television, commercial and photography industries. Our mission is to connect creatives with distinctive spaces while providing property owners with a seamless, profitable experience.

Who we are

With backgrounds in production, real estate and hospitality, our team understands the unique needs of both filmmakers and homeowners. We curate every listing with care, ensuring it not only looks fantastic on camera but functions smoothly for cast and crew.

Over the past decade we’ve grown our catalogue from a handful of homes to an expansive network of properties across California and beyond. We pride ourselves on transparency, integrity and exceptional service.

What we do

From scouting rare gems to coordinating permits, we handle every step of the location process. Productions benefit from our streamlined booking system and insider knowledge, while property owners rest easy knowing their homes are respected and insured.

Whether you need a mid‑century masterpiece, a sun‑drenched loft or an entire ranch, we’ll help you find it. Let us turn your vision into reality.
